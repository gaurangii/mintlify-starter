The **Business Hours** subsection allows you to set your organizationâ€™s working hours, including specific days and shifts (like night shifts).

## **How to set Business hours:**

1. Click on the "**Create Business Hours**" button to set business hours.

   ![182 Business Hours  Voxloud Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/business-hour/182.business_hours__Voxloud_resource_centre-1.png)
2. Fill out the **Name** field and add a short description.
3. Select the time zone and the business days.

   ![182 Business Hours  Voxloud Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/business-hour/182.business_hours__Voxloud_resource_centre-2.png)
4. Choose the corresponding start and end times for each working day.

   ![182 Business Hours  Voxloud Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/business-hour/182.business_hours__Voxloud_resource_centre-3.png)
5. If there is a night shift or additional shift, click on the **\+** icon to create a second shift.

   ![182 Business Hours  Voxloud Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/business-hour/182.business_hours__Voxloud_resource_centre-4.png)
6. Click **Add Business Hours** to save.

   ![182 Business Hours  Voxloud Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/business-hour/182.business_hours__Voxloud_resource_centre-5.png)
