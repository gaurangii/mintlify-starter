</Info>

In the **Departments** section, you can create and organize departments based on your needs. You can assign specific team members to a particular department.

## **How to create Department:**

1. Navigate to the **Departments** section.

   ![181 Departments  Voxloud Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/department/181.departments__Voxloud_resource_centre-1.png)
2. Enter a name for the department in the **Name** field.
3. Add a short description for the department, if needed.

   ![181 Departments  Voxloud Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/department/181.departments__Voxloud_resource_centre-2.png)
4. After filling in all the details click on the  "Add Department" button.

   ![181 Departments  Voxloud Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/department/181.departments__Voxloud_resource_centre-3.png)
