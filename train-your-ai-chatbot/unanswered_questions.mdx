### **How to see unanswered questions of your AI Chatbot?**

1. Log into your  Voxloud, and choose the bot you wish to analyse.

   ![27 Unanswered Questions  Voxloud Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/unanswered-questions/27.unanswered_questions__Voxloud_resource_centre-1.png)
2. Click on **“Train Your AI Chat Bot”** and go your AI Chat Bot.

   ![27 Unanswered Questions  Voxloud Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/unanswered-questions/27.unanswered_questions__Voxloud_resource_centre-2.png)
3. Click on the "**Unanswered Questions**'.
4. A list of all the unanswered questions will be visible on the screen. You can choose the date ranges for which the questions are to be shown, or download the same using the export button.

   ![27 Unanswered Questions  Voxloud Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/unanswered-questions/27.unanswered_questions__Voxloud_resource_centre-3.png)
5. Click on the icon on the right of the question to train the bot on it, directly from Unanswered Questions. Add the answer to the question, and then click on "Add FAQ" to add the FAQ

   ![27 Unanswered Questions  Voxloud Resource Centre 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/unanswered-questions/27.unanswered_questions__Voxloud_resource_centre-4.png)

### **FAQ's:**

Here are some FAQ's asked regarding Unanswered Questions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why are unanswered questions important for training?">
    They show what real users are asking but not getting answers to. Training on these helps your bot improve where it’s currently falling short.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the best way to respond to a missed question?">
    Add clear answers to your FAQ section or directly into your training data. You can also create a new category for repeated missed topics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often should I check for unanswered questions?">
    Weekly reviews are ideal—this keeps your bot updated with user needs and ensures continued accuracy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if unanswered question is too broad or vague?">
    You can rephrase it into a more specific, helpful FAQ and train the bot accordingly. Or add follow-up prompts to guide users better.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I train unanswered questions from the same tab?">
    Yes, unanswered questions can be trained from the same tab.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
