## **Why Facebook Chatbot?**

Creating a Facebook chatbot offers several benefits that can significantly enhance your online presence and business operations:

- **24/7 Customer Support:** You can respond instantly to customer inquiries at any time of the day.
- **Increased Engagement**: Chatbots can engage with your audience in real time, sending personalized messages, updates, and promotions.
- **Lead Generation**: By interacting with visitors and taking essential information, chatbots can help generate leads, making it easier to convert potential customers into actual customers.
- **Scalability**: Unlike human agents, chatbots can handle multiple conversations simultaneously.

To create your Facebook chatbot and manage all your chatbots, you first need to visit the  Voxloud Dashboard.

### **How to Create Your Facebook Chatbot?**

 1. Follow these quick steps to create your Facebook chatbot:-
 2. To start creating your new chatbot, click **"Create New Bot."**

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot Botpenguin Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-1.png)
 3. Select the primary purpose for creating your bot and proceed further.

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot Botpenguin Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-2.png)
 4. Select "**Facebook Chatbot**" to begin creating your chatbot.

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot  Voxloud Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-3.png)
 5. Now click on login and connect your facebook account.

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot  Voxloud Resource Centre 4 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-4.png)
 6. Enter your phone number/email address and click “Log in”.
 7. If you want to create a new Facebook account, click on “Create New Account”

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot  Voxloud Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-5.png)

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot Voxloud Resource Centre 6 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-6.png)
 8. Then select your business account which you want  Voxloud to access and usse chatbot for. You can opt for all the accounts or select a specific business account only.
 9. Then click on "continue".

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot  Voxloud Resource Centre 7 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-7.png)
10. You can check and review  Voxloud's access request and then click on save to move ahead.

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot  Voxloud Resource Centre 8 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__Voxloud_resource_centre-8.png)
11. After following the steps ahead your facebook bot will be ready to use.
12. You can now edit the flow for your bot. And you can now do customizations accordingly.

    ![9 Facebook Chatbot Botpenguin Resource Centre 9 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/create-newchatbot/9.facebook_chatbot__botpenguin_resource_centre-9.png)

Your Facebook chatbot is created.
