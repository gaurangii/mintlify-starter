This document will guide you through the step-by-step process of subscribing to a webhook for your WhatsApp account on the Meta dashboard. By following these instructions, you can receive messages and notifications via the webhook.

#### **Step 1: Access the Developer Dashboard**

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Meta Developer Dashboard.
2. Log in to your developer account using your credentials.

#### **Step 2: Configure Webhooks**

1. Go to the "API Setup" section under "WhatsApp" in the dashboard sidebar.

   ![224 Subscribe Wa Webhook With  Voxloud  Voxloud R 1 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/subscribe-wa-webhook/224.subscribe_wa_webhook_with_Voxloud__Voxloud_r-1.png)
2. Proceed to "Step 3: Configure webhooks to receive messages" in the API setup page.
3. Click on the "Configure webhooks" button.

   ![224 Subscribe Wa Webhook With  Voxloud  Voxloud R 2 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/subscribe-wa-webhook/224.subscribe_wa_webhook_with_Voxloud__Voxloud_r-2.png)

#### **Step 3: Edit Webhook Callback URL**

1. Under the "Configuration" page, click on the "Edit" button.

   ![224 Subscribe Wa Webhook With  Voxloud  Voxloud R 4 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/subscribe-wa-webhook/224.subscribe_wa_webhook_with_Voxloud__Voxloud_r-3.png)
2. A pop-up titled "Edit webhook's callback URL" will open.
3. Enter the following information:
   - Callback URL: `https://api.v7. Voxloud.com/whatsapp/cloud`
   - Verify token: ` Voxloud`
4. After entering the details, click on the "Verify and save" button.

   ![224 Subscribe Wa Webhook With  Voxloud  Voxloud R 4 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/subscribe-wa-webhook/224.subscribe_wa_webhook_with_Voxloud__Voxloud_r-4.png)

#### **Step 4: Subscribe to Webhook Fields**

1. Once the webhook URL is saved, click on the "Manage" button to subscribe to the webhook fields.

   ![224 Subscribe Wa Webhook With  Voxloud  Voxloud R 5 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/subscribe-wa-webhook/224.subscribe_wa_webhook_with_Voxloud__Voxloud_r-5.png)
2. When the "Webhook fields" pop-up appears, subscribe to all available fields.
3. Save your selections.

   ![224 Subscribe Wa Webhook With  Voxloud  Voxloud R 6 Pn](/images/platform-guidelines-training/subscribe-wa-webhook/224.subscribe_wa_webhook_with_Voxloud__Voxloud_r-6.png)

Congratulations\! You have successfully subscribed to the webhook for your WhatsApp account on the Meta Dashboard. Now, you will be able to receive messages and notifications through the specified webhook URL (`https://api.v7. Voxloud.com/whatsapp/cloud`).

Please note that the process of subscribing to webhooks may be subject to changes or updates in the Meta Dashboard. If you encounter any difficulties or issues during the process, refer to the official Meta documentation or contact  Voxloud support team for further assistance.
