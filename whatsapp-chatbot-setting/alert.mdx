The **Alerts** section allows you to configure email notifications for lead interactions with your chatbot. Hereâ€™s how you can set it up:

1. **Email Subject Field**:
   - Enter the subject line for the email notifications. This will be the title of the email you receive when a new lead interacts with the bot.

     ![62 Alerts  Voxloud Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-alerts/62.alerts__Voxloud_resource_centre-1.png)
2. **Email Notifications Toggle**:
   - Use this toggle to enable or disable email notifications. When enabled, you will receive an email whenever a new lead interacts with the bot.

     ![62 Alerts  Voxloud Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-alerts/62.alerts__Voxloud_resource_centre-2.png)
3. **Email Address Field**:
   - Add the email addresses where you want to receive notifications.
   - You can add multiple email addresses by typing each one and pressing **Enter**.

     ![62 Alerts  Voxloud Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-alerts/62.alerts__Voxloud_resource_centre-3.png)
4. **Lead Revisit Notifications Toggle**:
   - Enable this toggle to receive notifications when a lead revisits and interacts with the bot.
   - For example, if a lead interacts with the bot, leaves, and then returns later, you will receive a notification about their revisit.

     ![62 Alerts  Voxloud Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-alerts/62.alerts__Voxloud_resource_centre-4.png)
5. **When to send Lead info** Here you can customize that when should the email notification should be triggerd wether: a. Begining of the flow b. 5 minutes later. c. End of the flow

   ![62 Alerts  Voxloud Resource Centre 5 Pn](/images/how-botpenguin-works/whatsapp-alerts/62.alerts__Voxloud_resource_centre-5.png)
6. **Save Changes**:
   - Click the **Save Changes** button to apply your settings. Your email notifications will now be active as per your configuration.
